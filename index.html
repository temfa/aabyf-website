Who We Are

Founded in 1984, the All Africa Baptist Youth Fellowship (AABYF) is a department under the All Africa Baptist Fellowship (AABF); which is an arm of the Baptist World Alliance. We serve as a platform to unite and coordinate Baptist youth bodies on the continent for fellowship, missions, evangelism, church planting.

2

Our Purpose

AABYF is a dynamic, Mission and Impact-driven youth organization. We are committed to all-round development of capacity to influence the world for Christ and aim at connecting the world with the truth of the gospel of Christ.

3

Our Vision

To network, partner and strengthen fellowship amongst Conventions, Unions, Associations and centralized Baptist Churches in Africa to fulfil the Great Commission and engage in co-operative social action.

4

Our Reach

We are in over 42 countries in Africa across 57 Conventions and Unions and we operate a regional based leadership in service to God, our Conventions, Unions and Communities. Our sub regions are the Western, Central, Eastern, Southern Regions. We’re working to organize the Northern Region.
