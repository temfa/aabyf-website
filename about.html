About Us

The Body

Founded in 1984, the All Africa Baptist Youth Fellowship is department under the All Africa Baptist Fellowship with full as a regional Body of the Baptist World Alliance.

Our Spread

We are in over 42 countries in Africa across 57 Conventions and Unions and we operate a regional based leadership so as to take the gospel and impact to everywhere in Africa.

Our Focus

AABYF is a dynamic, Mission and Impact-driven youth organization. We are committed to alround development of capacity to influence the world for Christ and aim at networking the world with the truth of the gospel of Christ. We exist for fellowship, collaboration and Togetherness.
